Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpcStudiosLTD. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and games.

2. Information We Collect

When you play our games or use our apps, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Birth Details

3. Why We Collect This Data

We strictly use the collected data for internal, non-commercial security purposes, including:

  • Fraud Detection & Anti-Cheat Measures: To prevent hacking, exploitation, and unauthorized access to maintain a secure ecosystem.
  • Fair Gameplay: Ensuring a safe and equal playing field for all users in our multiplayer environments.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party companies, advertisers, or outside entities.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is safe with us, and you retain full control over it. We hold onto your personal details only as long as your account is active. If you choose to remove your account, you can use the "Delete Data" option inside the app. Once clicked, all of your collected personal information is immediately and permanently destroyed from our databases.

5. Advertisements and In-App Purchases

To keep our games running, we use monetization elements inside our apps:

  • Advertising: We display advertisements served by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may use anonymized device IDs or cookies solely to serve relevant ads.
  • In-App Purchases: We offer virtual goods and transactions. All financial details and payments are securely handled by the official app store (Google Play Store Billing), and we never see or store your payment details.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy or wish to make an inquiry regarding your data, please contact us at: spcstudiosltd@gmail.com.
